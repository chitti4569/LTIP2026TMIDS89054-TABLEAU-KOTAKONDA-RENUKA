Measuring the Pulse of Prosperity

An interactive analysis of the Index of Economic Freedom, examining how policy choices shape growth, opportunity, and global prosperity.

Explore Dashboards

Key Dimensions of Economic Freedom

Rule of Law

Evaluates property rights, judicial effectiveness, and government integrity.

Government Size

Measures tax burden, government spending, and fiscal health.

Market Openness

Assesses trade freedom, investment freedom, and financial freedom.

Project Overview

This project leverages Tableau visual analytics and the Flask web framework to present a data-driven exploration of economic freedom across countries and regions.

Users can interactively compare nations, analyze rankings over time, and gain insights into how institutional quality influences prosperity.
